# Art & Audio Direction

## Art

**Style:** Readable pixel / chunky low-res sprites (16–32 px characters) scaled up with
nearest-neighbor. Cel-friendly island colors (teal sea, warm sand, chalk white, pine green).

**UI:** Parchment panels, Cormorant Garamond for titles, Source Sans 3 for body — always
legible over dimmed backdrop.

**Scaffold approach:** Textures generated at runtime from canvas (no binary art required
to ship a playable build). Replace with hand-painted tiles later without changing APIs.

## Music

**Scaffold:** Procedural pentatonic / modal bed via Web Audio (gentle drone + sparse melody).
Replace with composed loops per region later.

**Inspiration (feel, not IP):** Wind Fish lullaby intimacy; Wind Waker sea cheer; OoT temple
motifs as "place memory."

## SFX

Short synthesized blips: sword whoosh, pickup chime, talk blip, hurt thud, UI confirm.
