# Design references

Lessons from Nintendo classics — **feel and structure only**. No assets, audio, names,
or story beats are copied.

## Link's Awakening (Wind Fish)

- Compact island that feels complete
- NPCs with routines and gossip
- Dream logic that still respects player agency
- Music as emotional spine

## Ocarina of Time

- Tools gate new sentences with the world
- Dungeons as arguments you can walk through
- Companion / mentor guidance without spoiling wonder

## The Wind Waker

- Horizon optimism; cel color clarity
- Charts and exploration fantasy
- Humor in townsfolk; sincerity in big themes

## Original IP note

Ninefold is original. Philosophies are public-domain schools of thought presented as
fictional island cultures. Mentors are invented characters.
