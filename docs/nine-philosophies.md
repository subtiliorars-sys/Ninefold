# The Nine Philosophies

Nine is the shape of this game: a 3×3 map of minds. Each school is a **region**, a
**mentor**, a **palette**, a **trial**, and a **Virtue Charm**.

Cultures drawn lightly from Mediterranean and Chinese island motifs — not historical
simulation. Mentors are folksy archetypes, not portraits of real people.

| # | School | Region vibe | Mentor | Virtue Charm | Trial seed |
|---|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **Stoa** | Cliff terraces, wind-worn columns | Aunt Calm | Still Heart | Stand in a storm without flinching (timing dodge) |
| 2 | **Garden** | Olive groves, soft laughter | Uncle Smile | Simple Joy | Escort bees to flowers; avoid greed-traps |
| 3 | **Academy** | Mirror pools, chalk stones | Sister Sketch | Clear Form | Arrange tiles into a shadow of truth |
| 4 | **Way** | Mist trails, bamboo & pine | Old Drift | Soft Path | Follow a river that refuses straight lines |
| 5 | **Harmony** | Rice terraces, family courtyards | Gran Courtesy | Right Relation | Seat guests so no one is slighted |
| 6 | **Inquiry** | Market of questions, lanterns | Cousin Why | Honest Doubt | Answer NPCs with questions that help them |
| 7 | **Craft** | Workshops, bronze & kiln | Smith Mean | Good Measure | Temper a blade by balancing heat puzzles |
| 8 | **Compassion** | Hospital gardens, quiet bells | Nurse Kind | Open Hand | Heal creatures instead of only fighting |
| 9 | **Emptiness** | High plateau, white sand circle | Silent Twin | Empty Cup | A room that only opens when you put items down |

## Map layout (mental 3×3)

```
  NW Stoa        N Academy      NE Way
  W Garden       C Agora Grove  E Harmony
  SW Inquiry     S Craft        SE Compassion
                 (Emptiness = highland above Agora)
```

Emptiness sits as a plateau overlooking the center — the "ninth fold" you climb toward.

## Dialogue tone

- Short, spoken lines (2–4 sentences max per click)
- Humor welcome; no dunking on belief systems
- Each mentor gives one **practice** the player can try in the overworld
